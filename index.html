About Me

I hold a Master's degree and two graduation degrees in the hospitality industry, evaluated by the World Education Society of Canada, along with a Red Seal certification. With a passion for excellence, I consistently meet and exceed performance goals. As the Director of Culinary Services, I work closely with the Executive Director to drive our strategic plan, annual budgets, and staff development. I ensure top-notch safety, quality, and client satisfaction in food services.With experience in supermarkets and strong leadership skills, I specialize in catering, food and beverage, hospitality management, and hotel management. Let's connect and explore opportunities for collaboration and growth!
